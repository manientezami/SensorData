Android multi-sensor dashboard

This page reads available browser-accessible sensors from an Android phone, including GPS, accelerometer, gyroscope, ambient light and barometric pressure where supported. It plots live accelerometer and gyroscope data and exports all captured samples to CSV. Open it in Chrome on Android over HTTPS or localhost. Sensor support depends heavily on the device and browser.
